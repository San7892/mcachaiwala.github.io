Our Story

Born From a Love of Coffee

It started in 2018 with a small roaster, a borrowed grinder, and an obsession with the perfect cup. What grew from a weekend farmers market stall is now a beloved neighborhood fixture.

We source our beans directly from small-scale farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Indonesia — building relationships that ensure quality and fair compensation for farmers.

Every drink is pulled, poured, and served with intention. We don't rush coffee, and we won't rush yours.
